About Stranger Things

Stranger Things is a Netflix original series set in the 1980s, blending supernatural horror with nostalgia. It follows a group of kids in the small town of Hawkins, Indiana, who encounter mysterious forces and government conspiracies. The show explores the eerie "Upside Down" and features beloved characters like Eleven, Mike, and Dustin.

Featured Episodes

Season 1

In Stranger Things Season 1, set in November 1983, Will Byers is abducted by a creature from the Upside Down. His mother, Chief Hopper, and friends search for him, while Eleven, a psychokinetic girl, escapes from a lab and helps them. The season focuses on rescuing Will and dealing with Hawkins Lab, spanning one week.

Season 2

In Stranger Things Season 2, set in 1984, Will Byers struggles with the aftermath of his time in the Upside Down. As strange occurrences escalate, the group faces new threats, including a dark entity from the Upside Down and the return of Eleven, who has been hiding and honing her powers. The season focuses on uncovering the growing danger and closing the rift between worlds.

Season 3

In Stranger Things Season 3, set in 1985, the group discovers a Russian lab under the Starcourt Mall and uncovers a new threat from the Upside Down. They must stop the danger while navigating growing up and changing relationships.

Season 4

In Stranger Things Season 4, set in 1986, the group is separated as new horrors emerge from the Upside Down. A powerful new villain, Vecna, targets Hawkins, and Eleven struggles to regain her powers while facing dark secrets. The team reunites to confront the growing threat and uncover the truth behind the Upside Down.
